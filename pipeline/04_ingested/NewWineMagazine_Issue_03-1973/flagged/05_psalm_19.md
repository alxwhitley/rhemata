# PSALM 19
*by Eugene W. Etheridge*

## Introduction to Psalm 19
The heavens proclaim God's glory everywhere
In universal language night and day.
His sun, a youthful runner, speeds his way
Dressed in a bridal-room, panoplied in air.

## The Law's Greater Message
The Law a greater message can declare,
More pure, complete, enduring it than they,
Its perfect wisdom drawing those astray.
Than honey is more sweet, than gold more rare.

## Personal Reflection
These shame my many imperfections, Lord.
How can I know them? Can them I understand
Presumptuous sins? By steadfast grace impart
Obedience deserving of reward,
And may I heed your just and true command
By spoken word and thoughts within my heart.
